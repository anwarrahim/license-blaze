Email Scraper Extension Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy aims to help you understand how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide or we obtain while using the Email Scraper Extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Our server gathers only basic statistical data, such as IP address, operating system type, and browser type, for analysis purposes.

Non-Personal Data

We only collect non-personal data like “aggregate” or “depersonalized” data from your usage, excluding any personally identifiable data.

Children's Privacy Protection

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Cookies Policy

The Email Scraper Extension does not use cookies directly to collect or store any information.

Software Usage

The Email Scraper Extension does not collect personal information. All saved data is stored locally on your device.

Information Disclosure

We may disclose information under specific circumstances, including legal requirements and protecting our rights.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, though no method of electronic storage is entirely secure.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Please review it periodically for changes.

Contact Us
